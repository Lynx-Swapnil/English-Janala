Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our organized lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes! Our platform offers free access to all basic lessons and learning materials. Premium features and advanced courses may require a subscription.

3. Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is optional but recommended. With an account, you can track your progress, save your favorite lessons, and access personalized learning recommendations.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

We provide a comprehensive vocabulary section with categorized word lists, interactive exercises, and spaced repetition techniques to help you learn and retain new words effectively.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we offer certificates of completion for our structured courses. You'll receive a digital certificate once you successfully complete all course requirements and assessments.
